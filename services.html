Business/Company Tax

Based on ATO regulations and the size of your business, quarterly or monthly Business Activity Statements (BAS) must be lodged. Our team works with you to ensure accurate and timely reporting of your financials to help you meet your business’ compliance obligations.

We assist with:

  • Financial Statements and Reports

  • Income Tax Returns

  • Monthly, Quarterly and Yearly BAS Preparations and Lodgements

  • Pay As You Go (PAYG) and Goods & Services Tax (GST) Reporting

  • Instalment Activity Statement (IAS) Submission and Variations

  • Capital Gains Tax
  • Tax Planning & Strategies

  • GST and PAYG Registrations and Cancellations

  • Negotiation with ATO Regarding Payment Plans and Waiving of Interest Charges/Penalties

Individual Tax Returns

At Business Class Solutions, we specialise in providing expert, one-on-one individual tax returns and advice to clients from an array of industries. Our aim is to get our clients the best possible return and find the best solutions for individual tax situations.

We handle all aspects relating to individual tax returns, including:

  • Tax Return for Individual Professionals

  • Sole Traders and Partnerships

  • Rental Properties

  • Investments and Capital Gains Tax

  • Foreign Income

We also offer online tax return services as well as express tax returns and late or prior year tax returns.

Self Managed Super Funds (SMSF)

Business Class Solutions are superannuation specialists. We provide administration, compliance, accounting and taxation services for Self Managed Super Funds (SMSF).

Our SMSF services include:

  • Set-up and Administration

  • Tax and Regulatory Advice

  • Financial Reporting and Compliance

  • Correspondence with the Australian Taxation Office (ATO)

  • SMSF Auditing by an Approved Auditor

  • Consultations, Fund Access, Wind-ups
